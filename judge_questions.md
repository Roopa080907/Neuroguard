# Judge Questions and Answers

## Why Spiking Neural Networks (SNN)?

SNNs mimic biological neurons by communicating through spikes.
They are more brain-inspired and can be more energy efficient than traditional neural networks.

## Why MRI scans?

MRI scans provide detailed brain imaging that can reveal structural changes associated with cognitive decline.

## What makes this project innovative?

The project combines dementia detection with Spiking Neural Networks, a neuromorphic computing approach inspired by the human brain.
