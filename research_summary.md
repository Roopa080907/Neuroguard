# Research Summary

## What is Cognitive Decline?

Cognitive decline refers to the gradual loss of memory, thinking ability, and decision-making skills. Early detection can help improve treatment outcomes and patient care.

## Why Brain Imaging?

MRI, fMRI, and PET scans can reveal structural and functional changes in the brain that may indicate cognitive decline.

## What are Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs)?

Spiking Neural Networks are brain-inspired neural networks that process information through spikes (events) rather than continuous signals.

## Why Use SNNs?

- Lower energy consumption
- Event-driven computation
- Inspired by biological neurons
- Suitable for neuromorphic hardware

## Expected Benefits

- Efficient analysis of medical images
- Reduced computational requirements
- Potential for real-time healthcare applications
